PRECIOUS TIMES

Childhood Years

1My name is Gab, and I was born and raised in Manila. 2I remember when I was in preschool, my grandmother used to escort me to the nursery school. 3I cannot recall if I did well in my studies, but I still remember the fun times when I had a tutor. 4I was a playful kid and had a really short attention span, so I guess my tutor had a hard time teaching me. 5After preschool, I was taken to the province to get my primary education. 6I only studied there for 1st grade, but there were a lot of memories. 7All the friends I made used to play with me within our relatives' compound. 8I remember the wounds I had when we played chase, hide and seek, and capture the flag. 9Back then, I did not have any technology. 10All I had were toys and books, but most of the time I just played with my toys. 11Another fun memory is when my grandmother told me to sleep during the afternoon so I could grow tall. 12However, I always ended up sneaking out of the house to play with my cousins. 13When I finished 1st grade, my parents took me back to Manila to study at the University of the East. 14During my first day I was scared and nervous because I had more classmates than in the province. 15I managed to make friends with a guy whom I cannot remember now, but we were good friends. 16After 2nd grade, I met this girl whom I am now still friends with till this day. 17This girl became like an older sister for me. 18I also got into a circle of friends that were nice and playful like me. 19My education back then was pretty good, but I remember failing art because I am not creative. 20A lot of teachers said I had wasted potential because of my attention span.

Teenage Years

1At the start of 7th grade, I was nervous because I didn't know anyone. 2I am an introverted teenager by nature. 3I can talk to people well if they start the conversation first. 4Luckily, the girl I’m friends with was my classmate again. 5Since I’m trying to get to know more people, I tried sitting with others in my row. 6I was very talkative, so the teacher transferred my seat to the last row. 7Most of my teenage years revolved around school, which gave me the best memories. 8I used to enjoy all the projects when the whole section was involved. 9The rehearsal and preparation of props and costumes were always so fun. 10During 8th grade, I had a new circle of friends that became my family. 11We created memes and posted them on a Facebook page that gathered ten thousand followers. 12Outside of school, I had a subgroup of friends who were huge anime fans. 13On a random week, it was announced that we would have a suspension due to a virus. 14When online classes started, I was unfamiliar since it was my first laptop. 15During that time, I became the president of a club. 16I tried my best to be among the With Honors students, which I actually achieved. 17In 12th grade, I started to take my life seriously and started going to the gym. 18I started having a fixed routine and a very organized schedule. 19I managed to finish senior high school and graduate as an ‘Honor’ student. 20I am grateful to make my parents get up the stage during my graduation.

College Years

1Entering my college year was terrifying and also very stressful at first. 2Although I was an ICT student, I was not confident with my programming knowledge. 3I was nervous that college topics would be far too complex for me. 4Luckily, it was not that hard, and most lessons were continuations of senior high. 5During my 1st year, my schedule allowed me to go to the gym and study. 6I learned things that became very interesting, like different programming languages. 7I was fascinated by how many languages can create similar programs. 8The history subject was also very interesting because of our professor. 9I didn’t join many organizations because I wanted to focus on my tasks. 10However, I did enjoy my NSTP program in civic welfare. 11I had a sense of purpose while helping the community. 12My PATHFIT classes were also a lot of fun for me. 13Sometimes we watched sports competitions which felt like a hangout. 14After my 1st year, things got a bit more complicated with the schedule. 15I had a lot of unnecessary vacancies and late evening dismissals. 16I used the vacant time to study and prepare for upcoming quizzes. 17My PATHFIT was enjoyable because the coach complimented my movement. 18My academic situation is decent, except for Discrete Mathematics. 19I was always bad at math, but thankfully I passed the subject. 20Hopefully, I will pass this 2nd semester despite the tiring schedule.
